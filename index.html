Introduction

Chalo apne shabdon (terms) ko define karte hain (samajhte hain). Is kitaab mein, main impossible (na-mumkin) shabd ka istemal do alag-alag (distinct) tariko se karta hoon. Ek hai bada I wala Impossible (capital I) aur dusra hai chhota i wala impossible (lowercase i). Capital I wale Impossibles wo cheezein hain jo pehle kabhi nahi ki gayi hain. Jab hum kisi koshish (endeavor) ko "impossible" kehte hain, toh aam taur par hamara matlab yahi hota hai. Sitaron (stars) tak jana. Hamesha ke liye zinda rehna. Udna. Light ki speed se bhi tez chalna. Reality (sacchai) ko modna. Soch ke dayre ya parampara (paradigms) ko badalna. Poore system ko puri tarah se theek/badal (overhaul) karna. Duniya ki bhookmari (world hunger) ko khatam karna. World champions banna.

Lowercase i wale impossibles wo cheezein hain jo aapko lagta hai ki aapke liye impossible hain. Ye wo sapna hai jise aapne chhod diya kyunki kisi ne kaha tha ki aap uske kabil nahi hain (weren't good enough). Ye wo business hai jise aap shuru karna chahte hain par abhi tak nahi kiya. Ye kisi purane sadme (past trauma) se bahar aana, kisi buri aadat ko todna, ya sofe (couch) se uthna hai. Aasan shabdon mein kahein toh, lowercase i impossibles wo cheezein hain jo humein apne liye ummeed se bilkul bahar (hopelessly out of reach) lagti hain, par poori insaniyat (humanity at large) ke liye nahi.

Ye kitaab is baare mein hai ki in dono ko haasil (accomplish) kaise kiya jaye. Ya is tarah kahein, kyunki lowercase i impossibles wo seedhiyan (stepping-stones) hain jo capital I Impossibles tak le jati hain, toh ye kitaab is baare mein hai ki pehle wale (former) par mastery haasil karke dusre wale (latter) ko kaise haasil kiya jaye.

Ye is baare mein bhi ek kitaab hai ki har kisi ko iski parwah (care) kyun karni chahiye. Is kitaab ka main vishay (thesis) ye hai ki hum sab usse kahin zyada karne ke qabil (capable) hain jitna hum jaante hain—aur ye sirf koi kahawat (metaphor) nahi hai. Ye ek biological fact (jaivik sach) hai. Teen dashakon (three decades) tak capital I Impossible ko padhne / study karne ka sabse buniyaadi sabak (fundamental lesson) ye hai ki...

Agar hum impossible ko haasil (accomplish) karna seekhna chahte hain, toh humein is baat se shuruat karni hogi ki hum apni motivation (prerna) ko apne khilaf nahi, balki apne fayde ke liye (work for us) kaise istemal karein. Biologically (jaivik roop se), motivation teen sub-skills (chhote kaushalon) ko milakar banne wala ek catchall term (mukhya shabd) hai: drive (junoon/lagan), grit (zidd/himmat), aur goals (lakshya).

Kyunki flow state ke liye bahut zyada energy (urja) ki zaroorat hoti hai, isliye motivation is operating system ka front end (shuruaati hissa) hai. Ye wo energy hai jo humein game mein (maidan mein) utarti hai.

Learning (seekhna) is puzzle (paheli) ka dusra hissa hai. Hum apne goals ko paane ke liye jis knowledge (gyaan) ki zaroorat hoti hai, use haasil karne ke liye motivation ki energy ka istemal karte hain. Halanki aisi laakhon cheezein hain jo koi seekh sakta hai, par impossible ki maang ye hai ki hum ye seekhein ki 'seekha kaise jata hai' (learn how to learn). Isliye, humein us biological system ko samajhna hoga jo knowledge haasil karne (knowledge acquisition) ko control karta hai.

Creativity (rachnatmakta / nayi soch) teesra kadam hai. Aksar, impossible ek impossible problem hoti hai. Jiska matlab hai ki humein ek naye (novel) solution (hal) ki zaroorat hoti hai. Biologically, creativity unhi naye solutions ko paida karne ka process hai. Ye is baare mein hai ki hum apne pattern recognition system (cheezon ko pehchanne wale system) ko kaise control karte (steer karte) hain taaki aisi ideas banaye ja sakein jo original (anokhe) aur useful (kaam ke) dono hon. Humein ye seekhna hoga ki is system ko apne fayde ke liye kaise chalaya jaye.

Aakhir mein, hamare paas flow hai. Flow ek amplifier (taqat badhane wala) hai. Ye pehli teen skills—motivation, learning, aur creativity—ko leta hai aur unhe ekdum unche level tak badha deta hai (turns them up to eleven). Flow consciousness (chetna) ki wo state hai jahan hum apna sabse best feel karte hain aur apna sabse best perform karte hain. Ye insaani performance ki sabse optimal state (behtareen avastha) hai.

Dhyaan dein ki is anukram (sequence) mein flow char mein se chauthe (number four of four) sthaan par aata hai. Par is kitaab mein, flow par section aakhiri hai, chautha nahi. Iska kaaran ye hai ki halanki motivation, learning, aur creativity khud mein alag-alag aur vishesh taur par ahem kshamataayein (important abilities) hain, par flow ke mamle mein, ye uske triggers bhi hain. Yani, wo condition (shartein) jo is state (avastha) ko paida karti hain. Dusre shabdon mein kahein toh, motivation ki madad se aap learning ko behtar (optimize) banate hain, motivation aur learning ki madad se aap creativity ko badhate hain, aur in teeno ki madad se aap flow ke maidaan ko bilkul behtareen (prime) bana dete hain.

Jab in chaaro ko ek sath laya jata hai aur ek sateek anukram (exact sequence) mein istemaal kiya jata hai, toh ye ek aisi biological machine (jaivik pranali) banate hain jo ummeed se badhkar nateeje (outsized results) deti hai. Yahi wajah hai ki sabhi impossibles shuru mein bilkul impossible hi lagte hain, jab tak ki koi unhe kar nahi deta—iske baad wo bas aage aane wali cheezon ke liye ek naya normal (naya aam standard) ban jate hain.

Lekin ye samajhna bahut zaroori hai ki flow koi aisi state (avastha) nahi hai jise aap zor dalkar (forcefully) paida kar sakte hain. Aap ye nahi keh sakte ki "aaj main flow mein rahunga" aur fir wo apne aap ho jayega. Flow ek result (parinaam) hai. Ye un halaton (conditions) ka nateeja hai jinhe aap apne aas-paas (environment mein) aur apne andar (psychology mein) banate hain. Aur un halaton ko banane ki shuruat motivation (prerna) se hoti hai.

Isiliye hum apna safar wahin se shuru karenge. Hum samjhenge ki motivation kya hoti hai, ye kaise kaam karti hai, aur sabse ahem baat, hum is biological force (jaivik taqat) ka istemal kaise kar sakte hain, na sirf apne aap ko aage badhane (propel) ke liye, balki us momentum (gati) ko lambe samay tak (long haul) banaye rakhne ke liye.

Kyunki impossible ko haasil karna koi chhota daud (sprint) nahi hai. Ye ek lamba marathon hai.

Aur marathon daudne ke liye, aapko ye seekhna hoga ki lambe waqt tak (over time) kaise lagatar aur mazbooti se chalte rehna hai (how to pace yourself). Iska matlab hai ki aapse jo ho sakta hai aur aapko jo lagta hai ki aap kar sakte hain, uski limit (had) ko push karna. Aur iske liye, humein thoda wild (junglee) hona padega.

...ki peak performance (sabse behtareen pradarshan) iske alawa aur kuch nahi hai ki hum apni biology (shaaririk pranali) se apne khilaf kaam karwane ki bajaye, use apne fayde ke liye (work for us) istemal karein.

Jo humein is kitaab ke structure (dhanche/banawat) par laata hai.

Ise practical (vyavaharik) hone ke liye design kiya gaya hai. Istemal kiye jane ke liye. Isliye, ye kitaab chaar hisson (parts) mein bati hui hai: Motivation (Prerna), Learning (Seekhna), Creativity (Rachnatmakta), aur Flow (Bahaav). Hum motivation se shuruat karte hain, jo peak performance ki buniyaad (foundation) hai. Fir hum learning ki taraf badhte hain, jo ye tay karta hai ki us motivation ka kya karna hai (kaise istemal karna hai). Uske baad creativity hai, jo ye tay karta hai ki raaste mein aane wali problems (pareshaniyon) ko hum kaise solve karein. Aakhir mein, hum flow par khatam karte hain, jo ye sikhata hai ki in sabhi ko amplify (bada) kaise kiya jaye.

Har section mein, hum us skill (kaushal) ki biology (jaivik vigyan) ko dekhenge jiski baat ho rahi hai, aur fir hum un tools aur techniques (tariko) ko dekhenge jo us skill ko train karne (sikhane) ke liye zaroori hain.

Maqsad (goal) aapko aapke khud ke impossible ke liye ek roadmap (rasta) dena hai.

Shuru karne se pehle ek aakhiri baat.

Ye peak performance ke baare mein ek kitaab hai, jiska matlab hai ki ye apni limits (hado) ko aage badhane (push karne) ke baare mein ek kitaab hai. Aur limits ko push karna hamesha uncomfortable (bechain karne wala) hota hai. Ise aisa hi hona chahiye. Agar aap uncomfortable nahi hain, toh aap apni limits ko push nahi kar rahe hain.

Toh uncomfortable hone ke sath aaramdayak (comfortable) mehsoos karna seekh lijiye.

Chaliye shuru karte hain.

Dopahar dhal chuki thi (late afternoon ka waqt tha). Mera bhai apne ek dost ke ghar se wapas aaya tha, usne Mom ko hello kaha, aur apni keechad se bhari (mud-spattered) jeans ki jeb se ek chamakti hui laal sponge ki ball nikali. Ye golayi mein kareeb ek inch ki thi aur iska rang bilkul fire truck jaisa laal tha.

Apne daahine (right) haath ki ungliyon par ball ko pakde hue, usne aaram se use apne baayein (left) haath mein rakha, uske chaaron taraf apni mutthi band ki, aur us band mutthi ko sabko dikhane ke liye upar utha liya. Kisi se—shayad mujhse, shayad Mom se—us par phook maarne (blow on it) ke liye kaha gaya.

Mom ne ye kaam kiya. Aur tab mere bhai ne apni ungliyan kholi aur mera dimaag hila diya (blew my mind). Wo ball gayab ho chuki thi. Mera matlab hai 'poof'. Gayab.

Mujhe poora yakeen tha ki mere bhai ne abhi-abhi impossible (na-mumkin) kar dikhaya tha.

Ab, zahir si baat hai, bahut se logo ke liye ek gayab hone wali sponge ball aisi koi khaas trick (jaadu) nahi hai. Par main nau (9) saal ka tha aur maine isse pehle kabhi haath ki safai (prestidigitation/magic) nahi dekhi thi. In halaton mein, "Abhi ye yahan hai, aur ab nahi hai" (Now you see it, now you don't) sach mein ek chakkar mein daal dene wala (baffling) experience tha.

Aur ye do wajahon se chakkar mein dalne wala tha.

Pehla, jo bilkul saaf tha: wo kambhakt ball gayab ho chuki thi. Dusra, jo thoda kam saaf tha: mera chhota bhai koi jaadugar (magic) nahi tha.

Is baat ka mujhe poora yakeen tha. Hamare ek sath bitaye gaye pichle saat saalo mein, usne abhi tak aisa kuch nahi kiya tha jo physics ke niyamo (laws of physics) ko todta ho. Kahin koi galti se hawa mein udne (levitation) wali ghatna nahi hui thi aur jab Dad ka favourite coffee cup gayab hua tha, toh kisi ne bhi mere bhai par ye ilzaam nahi lagaya tha ki usne use kisi aur dimension mein teleport kar diya hai. Toh bhale hi usne impossible ko kar dikhaya tha, agar mera bhai jaadugar nahi tha, toh iske peeche zaroor koi explanation (wajah/tark) hona chahiye. Shayad koi skill set. Shayad koi process (prakriya).

Ye ek hairaan kar dene wala ehsaas (startling realization) tha. Iska matlab tha ki impossible ka ek formula tha.

Aur zindagi mein maine jitna kisi aur cheez ko nahi chaha tha, usse kahin zyada main us formula ko janna chahta tha.

Jo is baat ko kafi had tak explain karta hai ki iske baad kya hua. . . .

Maine haath ki safai (prestidigitation) ko padhna/seekhna shuru kar diya. Card (taash) ki tricks, coin (sikke) ki tricks, aur yahan tak ki wo kambhakt sponge balls bhi. Jab main gyarah (11) saal ka hua, tab tak main ek tarah se Pandora's Box naam ki ek local magic shop par hi rehta tha. Aur maine Pandora's Box mein dher saare impossible dekhe.

1970s ke daur mein, magic (jaadu) apne sunehre daur (heyday) mein tha. Top ke magicians lagatar tour par jate the aur, aise kaarano se jo meri samajh ke bahar the, wo Cleveland, Ohio mein rukte the—jahan ye sab ghatna ghat rahi thi. Ye ek hadh se zyada achhi kismat (ridiculous good luck) thi. Iska matlab ye tha ki, der-saber (sooner or later), us (jaadu ki) duniya ka har bada naam meri is duniya mein aata tha. Nateeje ke taur par, mujhe impossible ko bilkul paas se (up close) aur har waqt dekhne ka mauka mila.
