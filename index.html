Introduction

The Formula for Impossible
(Impossible ka Formula)

"Mushkil wo hai jisme thoda waqt lagta hai; impossible wo hai jisme thoda aur zyada waqt lagta hai."
— Fridtjof Nansen

Ye kitaab un impractical (jo aam taur par vyavharik nahi soche jate) logo ke liye ek practical playbook (guide) hai.

Main "impractical" isliye keh raha hoon kyunki is kitaab ka subject hi 'impossible' (na-mumkin) hai. Ye un logo ke liye ek 'how-to' manual hai jo un cheezon ko haasil (accomplish) karna chahte hain jinke baare mein zyadatar logo ka manna hai ki unhe haasil nahi kiya ja sakta.

Ye ek chhota "i" wala impossible (small "i" impossible) ho sakta hai. Ye ek aisa kaam (feat) hai jo aapke liye impossible hai. Bachpan ka wo sapna jo aapne chhod diya kyunki aapse kaha gaya tha ki ye impractical hai. Kisi purane sadme (past trauma) se bahar aana. Kisi buri aadat ko todna. Ya bas aalas chhod kar sofe se uthna (Getting off the couch).

Ya fir ye ek bada "I" wala Impossible (capital "I" Impossible) ho sakta hai. Ye ek aisa kaam hai jo hum sab ke liye impossible hai. Wo cheezein jo pehle kabhi nahi ki gayi hain. Reality ko modna (Bending reality). Soch ke dayre ko badalna (Shifting paradigms). Chand par utarna. Chaar minute mein ek mile daudne ka record todna.

Mera vishwas, aur is kitaab ka main point (thesis) ye hai ki, hum sab usse kahin zyada karne ke qabil (capable) hain jitna hum jaante hain. Ye koi kahawat (metaphor) nahi hai. Ye ek biological fact (sach) hai.

Biologically, hum sab lagbhag ek jaise hi hain.

Iska matlab hai ki hamara hardware same hai. Hamara software same hai. Agar hum impossible ko haasil (achieve) karna chahte hain, chahe wo chhota "i" wala ho ya bada "I" wala, toh ek specific biological formula hai jise humein follow karna hoga.

Mere kaam ke results ekdum clear (saaf) hain: halanki hum kya possible (mumkin) maante hain ye ek insaan se dusre insaan aur ek culture se dusre culture mein alag hota hai, par use sach kar dikhane ka formula sabke liye same hai.

Ab, main maanta hoon ki, ye kitaab mukhya roop se (primarily) capital "I" wale Impossible ke baare mein hai. Maine pichle 30 saal un rare (bahut kam milne wale) logo ko padhne mein bitaaye hain jinhone is duniya ke sabse bade impossibles ko haasil kiya hai, ye samajhne ki koshish mein ki unhone kya kiya aur kaise kiya.

Aur mujhe ye pata chala ki halanki unke kaarname (feats) apne aap mein unique the, par unko chalane wale biological mechanisms (hamare sharir ke andruni system) unique nahi the.

In logo ke paas koi jaadu ki chhadi (magic wands) ya mutant genes (alag tarah ke DNA) nahi the. Wo koi superheroes nahi the. Wo bas aam log (ordinary people) the jinhone ye seekh liya tha ki apni biology (sharir aur dimaag ki pranali) ko apne khilaf nahi, balki apne fayde ke liye kaise istemal karna hai.

Unhone impossible ka formula decode (hal) kar liya tha.

Achi khabar ye hai ki aap bhi ise decode kar sakte hain.

The Formula (Formula)

Agar hum ye samajhna chahte hain ki impossible ko kaise haasil kiya jaye, toh humein insaani biology (human biology) ko dekhne se shuruat karni hogi.

Jab bhi hum impossible ko possible hote hue dekhte hain, chahe wo sports mein ho, science, art, technology, ya business mein, jo hum asal mein dekh rahe hote hain wo hamare biological systems ke ek specific set (jode) ka nateeja hota hai jo ek sath apne absolute maximum (sabse unche star) par kaam kar rahe hote hain.

Insaan ki is maximum performance wali state (avastha) ko hum 'flow' kehte hain.

Flow ko consciousness (chetna / dimaagi halat) ki ek optimal state (sabse behtareen avastha) ke roop mein define kiya jata hai, jahan hum apna sabse best feel karte hain aur apna sabse best perform karte hain.

...consciousness (chetna / dimaagi halat) ki ek aisi state jahan hum apna sabse best feel karte hain aur apna sabse best perform karte hain.

Ye term (shabd) University of Chicago ke psychologist Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi ne banaya tha. 1970s mein, Csikszentmihalyi ye samajhne ki koshish kar rahe the ki kyun kuch logo ki zindagi baakiyon se zyada meaningful (arthpurn) hoti hai. Unhone apni research un logo ka interview lekar shuru ki jo aisi cheezein karte the jinka koi sense nahi banta tha—kam se kam traditional, aarthik (economic) nazariye se toh bilkul nahi. Unhone rock climbers, modern dancers, chess players, aur jazz musicians se baat ki—aise log jo apna bahut bada waqt aur energy aisi activities mein laga dete the jisme unhe lagbhag na ke barabar paise milte the.

Unhe ye pata chala (He discovered) ki ye sabhi log ek hi feeling (ehsaas) ke peeche bhaag rahe the. Wo consciousness ki ek aisi state ke peeche bhaag rahe the jahan wo apna sabse best feel karte the aur sabse best perform karte the. Csikszentmihalyi ne is state ko "flow" kaha, kyunki jab unhone apne subjects se ye describe karne ko kaha ki is state mein kaisa feel hota hai, toh sabse aam jawab yahi tha, "Jaise kisi effortless (bina mehnat wali) nadi ki dhaara ke sath bahe ja rahe hon—ek flow."

Pichle 40 saalo mein, humne flow ke baare mein bahut kuch seekha hai. Hum jaante hain ki ye ek universal human experience hai (yani duniya ke har insaan ke sath hota hai). Ye kisi ke bhi andar, kahin bhi aa sakta hai, bas shart ye hai ki kuch shuruaati conditions (shartein) poori honi chahiye.

Hum ye bhi jaante hain ki flow ek spectrum (alag-alag levels ki range) par baitha hota hai. Is spectrum ke nakhle (low) end par us tarah ka micro-flow (chhota flow) hota hai jo aap tab experience karte hain jab aap kisi baatcheet ya email mein itna kho jate hain ki baaki sab kuch gayab sa ho jata hai. Is spectrum ke sabse high end par macro-flow (bada flow) hota hai, jahan waqt thehar sa jata hai (time dilates), khud ka ehsaas (sense of self) gayab ho jata hai, aur aapki performance aasman chhoone lagti hai (goes through the roof).

Lekin pichle 40 saalo mein humne flow ke baare mein jo sabse zaroori baat seekhi hai, wo ye hai ki ye insaani peak performance (sabse behtareen pradarshan) ki exact definition (paribhasha) hai. Is baat se koi farq nahi padta ki aap kya kar rahe hain, agar aap use apne sabse best level par kar rahe hain, toh aap flow mein hain.

Isiliye, jab main impossible ke formula ki baat karta hoon, toh main asal mein flow ki hi baat kar raha hoon. Flow wo biological mechanism (shaaririk pranali) hai jo humein impossible ko haasil karne ke kabil banata hai.

Aur flow ke impossible ka formula hone ka reason (karan) ye hai ki ye hamari physical (shaaririk) aur mental (maansik) dono kshamataon (capabilities) ko bahut bade level par badha deta hai (massive amplifier hai). Jab hum flow mein hote hain, toh hamari taqat (strength), sehan-shakti (endurance), aur reaction times badh jaate hain. Information ko process karne, patterns dhoondhne, aur problems ko solve karne ki hamari kabiliyat aasman chhoone lagti hai (skyrockets).

Flow state insaani kabiliyat ka sabse uncha shikhar (peak) hai.

Lekin ye aise hi hawa mein (in a vacuum) nahi hota.

Flow consciousness (chetna) ki ek state hai, aur consciousness ek biological process hai. Baaki sabhi biological processes ki tarah, flow ka bhi ek specific operating system hota hai. Ye system chaar alag-alag cognitive (dimaagi) abilities se milkar bana hai:

  1. Motivation (Prerna)
  2. Learning (Seekhna)
  3. Creativity (Rachnatmakta / Nayi soch)
  4. Flow (Pura dhyan / Bahaav)

Ye chaar abilities peak performance operating system ke components (hisse) hain.
